Our Swim Lessons

Choose the best program that fits your swimming goals.

Preschool Levels

Preschool 1

Age: 3-4 years

Skills: Introduction to water safety, basic floatation, and kicking skills. Children will become comfortable in the water and learn basic movements with assistance.

Preschool 2

Age: 4-5 years

Skills: Improved floatation, front and back gliding, and basic arm strokes. Children will begin to swim short distances independently with minimal assistance.

Preschool 3

Age: 5-6 years

Skills: Stronger arm strokes, breathing techniques, and basic stroke coordination. Children will be able to swim short distances with confidence and proper technique.

Ultra Levels

Ultra 1

Age: 6-7 years

Skills: Introduction to freestyle and backstroke, focusing on stroke technique, breathing, and building endurance over longer distances.

Ultra 2

Age: 7-8 years

Skills: Refining freestyle and backstroke techniques, learning the breaststroke and flip turns. Building endurance and swim speed.

Ultra 3

Age: 8-9 years

Skills: Advanced stroke techniques, endurance training, and introduction to competition style swimming.

Ultra 4

Age: 9-10 years

Skills: Perfecting all four strokes (freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, butterfly) with an emphasis on speed and efficiency. Introduction to competitive drills and race strategies.

Ultra 5

Age: 10-11 years

Skills: Continued improvement of techniques, focus on competitive racing, starts, turns, and finishes. Preparing for swim meets.

Ultra 6

Age: 11-12 years

Skills: Advanced racing techniques, pace setting, strategy building, and increasing endurance for longer race distances.

Ultra 7

Age: 12-13 years

Skills: Competitive swim training, strength building, and increased focus on specialized stroke development.

Ultra 8

Age: 13-14 years

Skills: Peak competitive training, advanced stroke techniques, race day preparation, and mental strategies for competition.

Ultra 9

Age: 14 years

Skills: Elite-level performance training for swimmers aiming for regional and national competitions. Advanced skills and strategies for success at high levels.
